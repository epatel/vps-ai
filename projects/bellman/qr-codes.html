For the proprietor

Bellman's Lost Epistle

A self-guided mystery walk across five Stockholm pubs.

How to use these cards:

  1. Print this sheet on A4 (one card per page).
  2. Cut along the outer border of each card.
  3. Post the matching card at each pub — bar, table-tent, or framed at the booth.
  4. Players scan the QR to begin that stop on their phone.

QR codes deep-link directly to each pub's page. Players can scan in any order — the linear unlock is enforced softly through the puzzle chain (no clue from stop N, no answer at stop 5). Latecomers and out-of-order groups still get a working stop page.
